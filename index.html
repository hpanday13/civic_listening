About the Project

Bridging the gap between lived experiences and data-driven urban planning

The Civic Listening project enhances urban planning by integrating thick anthropological data derived from observations, digitally documented experiences, and user feedback, with big data analytics to create inclusive and responsive public spaces.

Core Objectives

  • Developing new methods and technologies for including citizen voices in urban planning
  • Publishing case studies and toolkits on inclusive urban design

About ParkAble

ParkAble is our first pilot project. Prakable uses digitally documented park experiences from citizens to evaluate and ensure park accessibility and compliance with design standards.
